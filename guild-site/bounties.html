Bounties

Here’s this month’s list of available bounties. Please inform the guild hall before making any attempts to capture or kill any creature or humanoid on the list.

Shotragar the Fiendish

A red dragon causing trouble for various traders and merchants working around the Devil’s drylands. Rumor has it that she plans to take what supplies she has taken as well as her group of kobolds and lizardfolk and begin raiding nearby towns. A 4,000 gold bounty will be rewarded to the person or persons who stop her pilliaging.

Abnormal Owlbear

Reports from the Verdain Grove state that an owlbear of abnormally large size terrorizing travelers and the nearby town of Dunsbane. The people there are offering a 1,000 gold bounty for the removal of the creature and the guild itself is offering 300 gold for any information on how this owlbear came to be.

Ares Cooper

This dwarvish man was last seen taking a large caravan away from the town of Queen’s purchase. Reports say that he held up the entire caravan, taking everything of value from the passengers while ordering the caravan to continue moving. After doing so, the dwarf casted ‘flight’ on itself and flew out of the caravan. Tracing the magic he left behind yielded no results, as it seems that he only flew a short distance out of sight before leaving on foot. One of the victims is offering 700 gold for the return of their belongings.

Scar

The real name of this tiefling is currently unknown and is only described by its large scar on its face. It has been going around killing high ranking adventurers with a strange, unrecognizable magic. DO NOT ATTEMPT TO FACE THEM ALONE, as this man is dangerous.
